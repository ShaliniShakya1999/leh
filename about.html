ABOUT US
Infinity Routes is a leading integrated travel services company in the country, specializing in offering immersive experiences across the Himalayas. Our company is serving as a one-stop B2C platform for Ladakh, Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh & Uttarakhand.

The name "Infinity Routes" is built on a simple but powerful belief: travel is not just about reaching a destination — it's about endless possibilities, experiences and personal transformation. We provide our customers with a travel experience that reawakens the soul and kindles a renaissance within their lives.

Infinity Routes Travels commenced its operations in 2019 and has since emerged as one of the prime service providers offering expert local knowledge and logistical fulfilment. We specialize in:

  • Adventure tours
  • Cultural tours
  • Incentive groups
  • Special interest expeditions

We manage everything a customer needs for an immersive Himalayan experience.

Vision and Mission

To inspire a world where every journey is a transformation and every route leads to an infinite discovery of the self and the mountains.
