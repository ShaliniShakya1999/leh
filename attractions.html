Must-visit places in Leh Ladakh

Must Visit

Explore Ladakh

Pangong Lake

High-altitude lake with stunning blue waters and changing shades. A must-do Ladakh experience.

Nubra Valley

Sand dunes, double-humped camels & wide river valleys. Drive via Khardung La.

Khardung La Pass

One of the world's highest motorable roads (~5,359 m). Iconic for bikers and travellers.

Shanti Stupa

White-domed peace pagoda overlooking Leh. Stunning views at sunrise and sunset.
