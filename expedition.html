The Expedition

High-Altitude Motorcycle Adventure

The Ladakh Bike Expedition is a high-altitude motorcycle adventure crafted for thrill seekers who want to conquer some of the world's highest motorable roads while exploring Himalayan culture and landscapes.

Multiple departure dates (May – September 2026)

Day by Day

Detailed Itinerary

Tap any day to see what you'll do, where you'll stay and the highlights of that leg.

Day 1Manali ArrivalView details

Check-in at the hotel, freshen up and explore locally at your own pace. In the evening, gather at the hotel for a briefing by our expert on do's and don'ts and important information to make your trip memorable.

Overnight: Hotel at Manali

Day 2Manali to SarchuView details

After breakfast we head to Sarchu — a key stretch of the Manali–Leh Highway. We cross the Atal Tunnel and climb toward Baralacha La (4,890 m). After Baralacha La, the road descends toward Sarchu (4,290 m).

Overnight: Sarchu

Day 3Sarchu to LehView details

From Sarchu we pass Gata Loops (21 hairpin bends), Nakee La (4,740 m), Lachulung La (5,059 m), reach Pang, then cross Moore Plains and Tanglang La before reaching Leh in the evening.

Overnight: Hotel at Leh

Day 4Leh to NubraView details

Drive through Khardung La (~5,359 m). Visit Diskit Monastery. In the evening we reach Hunder. After check-in you can visit Hunder and take a camel safari at your own.

Overnight: Campsite at Hunder / Nubra

Day 5Nubra to PangongView details

Start early via the Shyok River route. Arrive at Pangong Tso, the famous high-altitude lake. Spend the evening by the lake and unwind at your lakeside camp.

Overnight: Cottages near Pangong Lake

Day 6Pangong to HanleView details

Ride through the Changthang region, lunch at Rizang La war memorial in Chushul, then ride toward Hanle. En route explore the raw beauty of the mountains and spot kiang (mountain donkeys).

Overnight: Guesthouse / homestay at Hanle

Day 7Hanle to Tso MoririView details

Start early from Hanle toward Tso Moriri via Umling La Pass — the world's highest motorable road. Spend time at Umling La, then continue to Tso Moriri, a breathtaking alpine lake in the Changthang Plateau.

Overnight: Tso Moriri

Day 8Tso Moriri to LehView details

Leave early from Tso Moriri to Leh. En route explore Chumathang (hot springs), lunch at Upshi. Visit Rancho School and Thiksey Monastery. Reach Leh in the evening.

Overnight: Hotel at Leh

Day 9Leh to KargilView details

After breakfast we set out for Kargil. Stunning views of the Moon Landscape near Lamayuru Monastery. We witness the confluence of Zanskar and Indus at Sangam. En route visit Magnetic Hill, Pathar Sahib Gurudwara and the Hall of Fame museum.

Overnight: Hotel at Kargil

Day 10Kargil to SrinagarView details

We visit the Kargil War Memorial and Mulbekh Monastery (statue of Chamba / Maitreya Buddha). Arrive in Srinagar for dinner at the hotel.

Overnight: Hotel at Srinagar

Day 11DepartureView details

After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Our team will ensure your smooth transfer to the airport for your onward journey. Take with you the thrill of adventure and unforgettable memories.
