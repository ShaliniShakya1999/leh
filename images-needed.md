## 📷 Content ke liye optional / improve – kya chahiye

Agar aap **content ke hisaab se** pages ko aur accha banana chahte ho, to neeche diye topics ke liye images add kar sakte ho. **Ye sab optional hai** — bina inke bhi site sahi chal rahi hai.

### 1. Home – Hero (Ladakh / bike focus)
- **Kya chahiye:** 1 strong image — Ladakh mountain **ya** bike expedition (rider + road).
- **Kahan use:** Home page sabse upar wala full-screen hero.
- **Suggest:** Khardung La / Pangong / Manali–Leh road pe bike ya landscape. Horizontal, wide crop (1920×1080 ya similar) best.

### 2. Destination pages (Leh, Nubra, Pangong) – extra variety
- **Abhi:** Har destination ke liye 1 hi image use ho rahi hai (hero + body dono).
- **Optional:** Agar chaho to har destination ke liye 1–2 **alag** photos (e.g. Leh – Shanti Stupa ek, market ek) use kar sakte ho; abhi same image do jagah repeat ho rahi hai.

### 3. Home – Srinagar card
- **Kya chahiye:** 1 image — **Srinagar / Kashmir** specific (Dal Lake, houseboat, ya Gulmarg).
- **Abhi:** "Kargil to Srinagar" road image use ho rahi hai.
- **Suggest:** Dal Lake, houseboat, ya Gulmarg — iconic Kashmir.

### 4. Home – Featured Package cards
- **Kya chahiye:** Har package ke liye 1 clear image.
- **Abhi:** Leh Ladakh Bike = Expedition Intro; Leh 3N/4D = Leh – Ladakh Package; Uttarakhand = Uttarakhand Tour; Ladakh Adventure Ride = same Expedition Intro.
- **Optional:** Agar har package ka alag highlight photo ho (e.g. 3N/4D ke liye Leh monastery, Uttarakhand ke liye Rishikesh/Mussoorie) to cards zyada distinct dikhenge.

### 5. Hotels & Villa Rental page
- **Kya chahiye:** 1–2 photos — hotel room, villa, ya camp (Ladakh / Manali / Srinagar).
- **Kahan use:** Page hero ya "Where You Stay" type section.
- **Suggest:** Apne kisi partner hotel / camp ki real photo.

### 6. Religious Tours page
- **Kya chahiye:** 1 image — temple, Char Dham, monastery ya yatra scene.
- **Kahan use:** Page hero ya "Sacred Destinations" section.
- **Suggest:** Kedarnath, Badrinath, Hemis, ya koi pilgrimage route.

### 7. Uttarakhand / Nepal (agar alag destination cards chahiye)
- **Kya chahiye:** 2 images — Uttarakhand (Mussoorie/Rishikesh/Valley of Flowers) + Nepal (mountain/temple).
- **Kahan use:** Home "Popular Destinations" me agar 5–6 cards ki jagah 7–8 karna ho.
