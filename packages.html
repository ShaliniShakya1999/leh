Ladakh Bike Expedition 2026

Our Ladakh Bike Expedition is a high-altitude motorcycle adventure designed for thrill seekers and explorers. This journey combines adventure, safety, and unforgettable experiences across the majestic Himalayas.

From Srinagar to Manali, you will ride through iconic mountain passes, pristine lakes, remote villages, and breathtaking landscapes — Khardung La, Umling La, Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, Hanle and Tso Moriri.

This itinerary includes all destinations, accommodation, daily schedule, and pricing so your expedition runs smoothly.

Destination Overview

  • Leh — Central base for rest, acclimatization, and local exploration. Visit monasteries, markets, and scenic viewpoints.
  • Khardung La Pass — One of the world's highest motorable passes (~5,359 m); an iconic ride for adventure bikers.
  • Nubra Valley — Famous for sand dunes, double-humped camels, and wide river valleys.
  • Pangong Lake — A high-altitude lake (~4,350 m) with stunning blue waters, changing shades, and breathtaking scenery.
  • Hanle — A remote village known for its astronomical observatory and pristine landscapes.
  • Tso Moriri Lake — A serene, high-altitude lake (~4,522 m) surrounded by mountains and wildlife.
  • Umling La Pass — One of the highest motorable passes in the world (~5,800 m); the ultimate riding challenge for adventure seekers.

Route Plan

Srinagar to Manali

11-day bike expedition: Srinagar → Kargil → Leh → Nubra → Pangong → Hanle → Tso Moriri → Sarchu → Manali. Each day is planned for safety, acclimatization and the best views.

Day 1

Srinagar Arrival

Check-in at the hotel, freshen up and explore Srinagar at your own pace. In the evening, gather for a briefing by our expert on do's and don'ts and important information to make your trip memorable.

Overnight: Srinagar (Hidden Leaf / Olive Greens)

Day 2

Srinagar to Kargil

After breakfast we head to Kargil. En route visit Mulbekh Monastery with the captivating statue of Chamba (Maitreya Buddha). We also pay tribute at the Kargil War Memorial. Arrive in Kargil for dinner and rest.

Overnight: Kargil (Rangyul Resort)

Day 3

Kargil to Leh

Ride from Kargil to Leh. We witness the Moon Landscape near Lamayuru Monastery, the confluence of Zanskar and Indus at Sangam, Magnetic Hill, Pathar Sahib Gurudwara and the Hall of Fame museum. Reach Leh in the evening for acclimatization.

Overnight: Leh (Hotel Leh Chen)

Day 4

Leh to Nubra via Khardung La

Ride through one of the most famous passes — Khardung La (~5,359 m), once the world's highest motorable road. Visit the impressive Diskit Monastery. In the evening we reach Hunder for tonight's stay. After check-in you can visit Hunder and take a camel safari at your own.

Overnight: Nubra (Boutique Camps)

Day 5

Nubra to Pangong

Start early via the beautiful and remote Shyok River route. Arrive at Pangong Tso, the famous high-altitude lake known for its ever-changing hues. Spend the evening by the lake and unwind at your lakeside camp.

Overnight: Pangong (Barleys Camp)

Day 6

Pangong to Hanle

We ride through the Changthang region and have lunch at the Rizang La war memorial in Chushul, then ride toward Hanle. En route explore the raw beauty of the colourful mountains and spot the mountain donkeys known as kiang. Stay in guesthouses or homestays in Hanle.

Overnight: Hanle (AMA Cottages)

Day 7

Hanle to Tso Moriri via Umling La

Start early from Hanle toward Tso Moriri via Umling La Pass — the world's highest motorable road (5,798 m). Spend time at Umling La, then continue to Tso Moriri, a breathtaking alpine lake in the Changthang Plateau, known for its stunning blue shades and rich wildlife.

Overnight: Tso Moriri

Day 8

Tso Moriri to Leh

Leave early from Tso Moriri to Leh. En route explore Chumathang (hot springs) and have lunch at Upshi. Visit Rancho School and Thiksey Monastery. Reach Leh in the evening. Explore local cafés at Leh market at your own.

Overnight: Leh (Hotel Leh Chen)

Day 9

Leh to Sarchu

After breakfast we set out for Sarchu on the Manali–Leh Highway. We cross Tanglang La, ride through Moore Plains, pass Lachulung La (5,059 m), Nakee La (4,740 m) and Gata Loops (21 hairpin bends) before reaching Sarchu (4,290 m).

Overnight: Sarchu (Himalayan Spirit Camps)

Day 10

Sarchu to Manali

After breakfast we head to Manali. We climb toward Baralacha La (4,890 m), then descend through the Atal Tunnel to reach Manali in the evening. This completes the Srinagar–Manali bike expedition.

Overnight: Manali (Mapple Retreat)

Day 11

Departure

With cherished memories, it's time to bid farewell to Ladakh. After breakfast, check out from the hotel. Our team will ensure your smooth transfer to the airport for your onward journey. Take with you the thrill of adventure, the serenity of Ladakh, and a treasure trove of unforgettable moments.
