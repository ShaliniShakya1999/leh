Policies

Clear terms for your Ladakh Bike Expedition — what’s included, payments, cancellation and safety

Key terms for your Ladakh Bike Expedition. Please read before booking.

Inclusions Exclusions Payment Cancellation Important Notes

Inclusions

  • Royal Enfield Himalayan 411cc bike for the trip
  • Fuel as per itinerary
  • Backup vehicle, luggage van & support crew
  • All permits (inner-line etc.)
  • Accommodation (triple-sharing as per itinerary)
  • Breakfast (Day 2–11) & Dinner (Day 1–10)
  • First aid kit & oxygen cylinder
  • Complimentary Volvo (Manali–Delhi) at trip end
  • Riding jackets & helmets (rider & pillion)

Exclusions

  • Adventure activities (paragliding, rafting, etc.) unless specified
  • Tips, security deposit for bike/gear
  • Medical costs, evacuation, travel insurance
  • Transport outside the itinerary
  • Entry/camera fees, personal expenses (laundry, alcohol, shopping)
  • Costs from unforeseen events (landslides, road blocks, weather)
  • GST (5%) on tour cost
  • Anything not listed under Inclusions

Payment Terms

  • At booking: Non-refundable booking amount to secure your seat
  • 30 days before departure: 50% of remaining balance
  • Day 1 of trip: Final balance before expedition starts

Bank transfer, UPI or methods we share at booking. All amounts in INR. GST applies on total cost.

Cancellation Policy

  • Booking amount: Non-refundable in all cases.
  • More than 30 days before departure: Only booking amount forfeited; rest refunded (minus any bank charges) within 14 working days.
  • 15–30 days before: 50% of tour cost (minus booking amount) forfeited; remainder refunded within 14 working days.
  • Less than 15 days before: No refund. Reschedule to a future batch may be offered subject to availability.
  • If we cancel: (safety, weather, government/force majeure) — full refund or reschedule; refund within 14 working days.

Cancel in writing (email or WhatsApp). Refunds to same account/mode used for payment.

Important Notes

  • Baggage: Rucksack/duffel only (no trolley bags). ₹500/kg penalty for trolley bags.
  • Rider: Valid Indian motorcycle licence, min. age 18, reasonable riding experience. Use provided helmet & jacket; follow group leader.
  • Health: Consult your doctor if you have heart/lung/BP issues. We carry first aid & oxygen; serious cases may need evacuation at your cost.
  • Travel insurance: Not included. We recommend cover for medical, evacuation, trip cancellation and high-altitude/motorcycle travel.
  • Liability: We are not liable for injury, loss or damage beyond applicable law; nor for incidents outside organised activities, or delays/costs from events beyond our control. Bike/gear damage from negligence may be charged.
  • Tour extension / breakdown: Extensions charged separately. Transport breakdown: group waits for repair; alternate transport only if arranged at extra cost.
  • Volvo: Manali–Delhi bus via third-party; service issues to be taken up with operator (we share contact).
  • Media: Photos/videos you share may be used for our marketing; no compensation.
  • Conduct: Respect local culture; no littering. No narcotics/weapons; non-compliance means exclusion with no refund.

Questions about our policies? We’re happy to clarify.
