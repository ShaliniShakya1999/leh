Policies

Inclusions, exclusions, payment & cancellation

Inclusions

  • Royal Enfield Himalayan 411cc
  • Fuel (as per itinerary)
  • Mechanical support
  • Backup vehicle
  • Luggage vehicle
  • Group leader
  • All permits
  • Accommodation (triple sharing)
  • Breakfast (Day 2 to Day 11)
  • Dinner (Day 1 to Day 10)
  • General first aid
  • Oxygen cylinder
  • Complimentary Volvo (Manali to Delhi)
  • Complimentary riding jackets & helmets (rider & pillion)

Exclusions

  • Adventure activities
  • Tip for supporting team
  • Security deposit for bike and riding gear (refundable)
  • Medical or evacuation expenses
  • Transport apart from the itinerary
  • Entry tickets, video/still camera charges
  • Personal expenses (tips, calls, laundry, alcohol, beverages)
  • Costs due to unforeseen circumstances (landslides, road blocks, weather)
  • Travel insurance
  • Anything not mentioned under inclusions
  • 5% GST

Payment Policy

  • Booking amount payable at time of booking.
  • Remaining 50% within 30 days of departure.
  • Total balance on 1st day of the trip.

No refund is applicable in any type of cancellation or circumstances.

Cancellation Policy

  • Booking amount is non-refundable.
  • Cancellations must be made in writing.
  • If Infinity Routes Travels cancels due to weather, safety or government restrictions, rescheduling will be offered.
  • Refunds (if applicable) are processed within 7–14 working days.

Things to Remember

  • Extension of tour due to any circumstances — extra charges paid directly by the client.
  • GST is applicable on any payment mode (cash/online), even after negotiated amount.
  • Narcotics and banned substances are strictly prohibited during the tour.
  • Weapons, fireworks and toxic substances are not allowed.
  • We are not responsible for your safety outside camping premises. Loss/damage to camp materials will be charged at MRP.
  • No responsibility for injuries or loss/theft of personal property.
  • Not responsible if you fall ill while travelling.
  • Transport breakdown en route — you wait until repaired; no backup transport provided.
  • Cost to transfer/tow the motorbike if dropped en route is excluded.
  • Not responsible for delays due to traffic, natural calamities or breakdown.
  • Plan may change due to snowfall or other natural calamities.
  • Bonfire and musical nights subject to local authority/camp/hotel rules.
  • Bus/traveler seats on first-come-first-serve basis.
  • Volvo provided through third-party; service issues to be taken up with the supplier directly.
  • Please cooperate in keeping the environment clean and safe.

Baggage

Trolley bags are not allowed. Carry only rucksack bags. A penalty of ₹500 per kg will be charged if you do not have rucksack bags.
