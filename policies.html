Policies

Clear terms for your Ladakh Bike Expedition — what’s included, payments, cancellation and safety

Please read these policies before booking. They outline what is included in your expedition, payment and cancellation terms, rider requirements, and important safety information. We are committed to transparency and your safety.

Inclusions

Your expedition package includes the following:

  • Royal Enfield Himalayan 411cc motorcycle for the duration of the trip
  • Fuel as per the published itinerary
  • Mechanical support and backup vehicle throughout the route
  • Luggage vehicle for your belongings
  • Experienced group leader and support crew
  • All required permits and inner-line permits
  • Accommodation on triple-sharing basis (as per itinerary)
  • Breakfast (Day 2 to Day 11) and Dinner (Day 1 to Day 10)
  • Basic first aid kit and oxygen cylinder for high-altitude support
  • Complimentary Volvo bus transfer (Manali to Delhi) at the end of the expedition
  • Complimentary riding jackets and helmets for rider and pillion

Exclusions

The following are not included in the package price:

  • Adventure activities (paragliding, river rafting, etc.) unless specified
  • Tips or gratuities for the support team
  • Refundable security deposit for the motorcycle and riding gear
  • Medical expenses, evacuation costs or travel insurance
  • Any transport outside the published itinerary
  • Entry fees, camera charges at monuments or parks
  • Personal expenses (calls, laundry, alcohol, beverages, shopping)
  • Costs arising from unforeseen circumstances (landslides, road blocks, weather delays)
  • Travel insurance (strongly recommended — see Travel Insurance section)
  • Applicable GST (5%) on the tour cost
  • Anything not explicitly mentioned under Inclusions

Payment Terms

To confirm your booking, we follow a simple payment schedule:

  • At the time of booking: A non-refundable booking amount (as communicated at the time of enquiry) is required to secure your seat.
  • 30 days before departure: 50% of the remaining balance must be paid.
  • On Day 1 of the trip: The final balance is payable before the expedition begins.

Payments can be made via bank transfer, UPI or other methods we share at the time of booking. All amounts are in Indian Rupees (INR). GST is applicable on the total tour cost.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation terms are as follows:

  • Booking amount: The initial booking amount is non-refundable in all cases.
  • Cancellation by you: Cancellations must be communicated to us in writing (email or WhatsApp). Depending on when you cancel, the following applies:
    • More than 30 days before departure: Only the booking amount is forfeited; any additional amount paid may be refunded (excluding bank/processing charges, if any) within 14 working days.
    • 15–30 days before departure: 50% of the total tour cost (minus booking amount) may be forfeited; the remainder, if any, will be refunded within 14 working days.
    • Less than 15 days before departure: No refund is applicable. We may offer a reschedule to a future batch subject to availability.
  • Cancellation by Infinity Routes Travels: If we cancel the expedition due to safety, weather, government restrictions or force majeure, we will offer you a reschedule to another date or a full refund (including booking amount) within 14 working days.
  • Refund timeline: Eligible refunds are processed within 7–14 working days to the same account/mode used for payment.

Rider Requirements

For your safety and the safety of the group, we require:

  • Valid motorcycle driving licence (valid in India) for the rider
  • Reasonable riding experience on mountainous or long-distance routes; this expedition involves high-altitude passes and challenging terrain
  • Minimum age 18 years for riders; pillions may be younger subject to parent/guardian consent
  • Physical fitness to undertake long riding days and high altitude (we recommend a health check if you have any concerns)
  • Adherence to the group leader’s instructions and route plan at all times
  • Use of the provided safety gear (helmet, jacket) throughout the ride

Medical & Safety Guidelines

Your wellbeing is our priority. Please note:

  • We carry a basic first aid kit and oxygen cylinder. We are not a medical service; serious illness or injury may require evacuation at your cost.
  • If you have a pre-existing medical condition (heart, lung, blood pressure, etc.), please consult your doctor before booking and inform us in confidence.
  • Stay hydrated, avoid alcohol at high altitude and follow our team’s advice on acclimatisation.
  • Itinerary may be modified by the group leader for safety (weather, road conditions, health of participants).
  • We do not allow narcotics, weapons or banned substances on the tour. Non-compliance will result in immediate exclusion with no refund.

Liability Disclaimer

By participating in the expedition, you acknowledge:

  • Infinity Routes Travels and its team are not liable for injury, loss of life, or damage to personal property arising from your participation, except where required by applicable law.
  • We are not responsible for incidents that occur outside our organised activities (e.g. when you are not with the group or at the camp/hotel).
  • We are not liable for delays, route changes or additional costs caused by factors beyond our control (natural calamities, government orders, road blocks, transport breakdown).
  • Damage or loss to the motorcycle or provided gear due to rider negligence may be charged as per our terms shared at the start of the trip.

Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend that you obtain comprehensive travel insurance covering:

  • Medical emergencies and evacuation from high-altitude regions
  • Trip cancellation or curtailment
  • Loss or theft of personal belongings
  • Personal accident cover during the expedition

Travel insurance is not included in the package and is your responsibility. Please ensure your policy is valid for motorcycle tours and high-altitude travel in India.

Important Notes

  • Baggage: Only rucksack/duffel bags are permitted (no trolley bags). A penalty of ₹500 per kg may apply if you bring trolley bags, as they are unsuitable for the luggage vehicle.
  • GST: Applicable on all payments regardless of mode (cash/online).
  • Tour extension: Any extension of the tour due to your request or circumstances will be charged separately and paid directly by you.
  • Transport breakdown: In case of breakdown of our transport en route, the group will wait until the vehicle is repaired. We do not provide alternate transport for the same leg unless arranged at extra cost.
  • Volvo/bus: Complimentary Volvo (Manali–Delhi) is provided through a third-party operator. Any service issues must be taken up with the operator; we will assist with contact details.
  • Media: Photos or videos you share with us may be used for our website, social media and marketing. By sharing, you grant us a royalty-free licence for such use. No compensation or copyright claim applies.
  • Environment: We request all participants to respect local culture and keep the environment clean. Littering is not permitted.
