Things to Carry

Pack smart for the Ladakh Bike Expedition

Checklist

What to Pack

🧥

Clothing

  • Base layer: wicking underwear
  • Mid layer: insulating jacket/vest/pants
  • Dry clothing after ride
  • Insulating hat, cap or headband
  • Face mask or balaclava
  • Goggles
  • Waterproof over-mittens
  • Rain coat (compulsory)
  • Gloves or mittens
👟

Footwear

  • Insulated boots
  • Trekking shoes with high sole
  • Casual shoes
  • Main socks, liner socks, spare socks, woollen socks
📄

Documents

  • Undertaking certificate (download format from us)
  • Identity card
🥜

Nutrition

  • Dry fruits
  • Energy bars
  • Energy beverages
